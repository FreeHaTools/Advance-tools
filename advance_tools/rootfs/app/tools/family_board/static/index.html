How the Family Board works

🛒 Lists

Lists are real Home Assistant to-do lists — not something private to this add-on. Anything you add here instantly appears in the HA mobile app (To-do lists section) and can be used by voice assistants ("add milk to the shopping list"). Anything added from the phone or by voice shows up here within seconds.

  • Tap the checkbox to mark an item done — it moves into the collapsible Done section.
  • Tap an item's text (or the ✏️ icon) to rename it.
  • Clear completed permanently removes everything in Done.

🧹 Chore rotation

Each chore has a list of people who take turns. When someone taps Mark done, the turn passes to the next person in the list.

Chore “Dishes 🍽️” with people Mom → Dad → Sam:
Monday it says Today: Mom. Mom marks it done → Tuesday it says Today: Dad. After Sam it wraps back to Mom.
The 🔥 streak counts how many due days in a row it was actually done.

Pick specific weekdays for a chore (e.g. Trash only on Tuesdays) or leave all days off to make it a daily chore. "Due today" resets every midnight.

📌 Notes

Sticky notes are quick family reminders ("Grandma visits Sunday 🎂"). They live only in this add-on, autosave when you tap away, and can be recolored with the dots at the bottom of each note.

  • Audience — the badge on each note decides who sees it on dashboards: 🌍 Everyone, or 👤 specific users. Users are the accounts people and wall tablets log in with, managed in Hub → Users. Notes always stay visible here for admins.
  • Replies — family members can answer a note straight from their dashboard ("Done!", "Buy 2 please"). Replies show under the note here; you can reply back or remove any reply with ✕. A user can also remove their own reply from the dashboard.
Note “Feed the cat before school 🐱” targeted at kids_tablet only shows on the tablet logged in as kids_tablet — the kids tap reply “done ✔” and you see it here.

🖥️ On dashboards

Notes and shopping lists can be placed on end-user dashboards with the Family Notes and Shopping List widgets in the Dashboard Maker designer. What a dashboard can show is filtered twice:

  • Notes follow their audience — a dashboard only shows notes targeted at the user it is logged in as (or at everyone).
  • Lists follow the dashboard's entity allowlist — only todo.* entities allowed on that dashboard appear, and items can be added/checked/cleared right from the tablet. Wildcards like todo.* work in the allowlist.

💡 Tips

  • Put this board on a wall tablet — create a kiosk dashboard with Dashboard Maker and keep the tablet logged in.
  • Give each tablet its own login (e.g. kitchen_tablet, kids_tablet) so targeted notes land on the right screen.
  • Voice: "Hey Google / Alexa / Assist — add eggs to Shopping."
  • New lists need the Local to-do integration (built into every recent Home Assistant). If creating a list fails, update HA Core.
