How Entity Doctor works

1
Scan. Every entity and device is checked against the registry and live states. Nothing is changed by a scan — it only looks.
2
Understand the categories. Click a box to see its entities:
💀Dead devices — every entity of the device is unavailable. The device probably lost power, broke, or was removed from your home.
👻Orphaned — registry leftovers no integration provides anymore. Always safe to delete.
🔌Unavailable / ❓ Unknown — currently not reporting. Often fixed by reloading the integration (the 🔧 Repair button does this for you).
🪫Low battery / 🕸️ Stale — early warnings that hardware needs attention.
3
Check usage before deleting. The 🔎 button shows every automation, script and scene that references an entity, so you never break one by accident.
4
Clean up with Triage. Open 🗂 Triage and drag cards: Keep remembers your choice, Clean up queues a deletion. Nothing runs until you press Apply and confirm the review list.
5
Everything is logged. 📜 Log shows every deletion this tool ever made, with timestamps. Live entities are never deleted — at most disabled, which is reversible.
📜

Deletion log

Every entity and device this tool removed, newest first. Deletions cannot be undone, but this log always tells you what happened.
