🤖 Your Telegram bot

A bot is a free Telegram account that belongs to you. Notify Hub uses it to send messages — and, if you let it, to answer commands from your phone.
  1. In Telegram, open a chat with @BotFather.
  2. Send /newbot and follow the two questions (a name and a username ending in bot).
  3. BotFather replies with a token like 123456789:AAE… — paste it below and press Save.
  4. Open a chat with your new bot and send it /start. Then press Find my chats to pick up your chat ID.

💬 Who may command the bot

Only these chat IDs can use commands. Anyone else gets a polite refusal — and is told their own chat ID, so you can add them here on purpose. Group chats work too: add the bot to the group, send /start there, then press Find my chats.

🎛️ Quick controls

Devices picked here appear as buttons when you send /control to the bot. Tapping one toggles it — nothing else can be controlled from Telegram, so pick only what you trust.
